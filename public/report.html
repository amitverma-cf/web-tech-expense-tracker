Introduction

ExpenseTrack Pro is a comprehensive personal finance management application designed to help users track their income, expenses, set budgets, and generate insightful reports for better financial decisions.

Problem Statement

Managing personal finances without proper tools leads to overspending and poor financial health

Solution

A user-friendly web app that provides real-time tracking, budgeting, and visual analytics

Target Users

Individuals, students, and professionals who want to take control of their finances
